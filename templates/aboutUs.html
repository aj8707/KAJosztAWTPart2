Our History

Established in 2010, our motorsports quiz website has a rich history steeped in the love for racing and a commitment to quizzing excellence. The brainchild of passionate motorsports enthusiasts, the website was born out of a desire to create a platform that celebrated the history, diversity, and excitement of the racing world.

Over the years, we evolved, constantly refining our content to encompass the ever-expanding realm of motorsports, from Formula 1 and MotoGP to rallying and endurance racing. Our quizzes became a go-to destination for fans seeking both entertainment and education, with carefully curated questions that spanned the decades of racing history.

In 2015, a major website redesign marked a significant milestone, enhancing user experience and introducing new features to engage our growing community. As the motorsports landscape continued to evolve, so did we, staying at the forefront of quiz innovation.

The website's commitment to accuracy and relevance led to collaborations with industry experts and insiders, ensuring our quizzes remained a trusted source for both seasoned aficionados and newcomers alike. Today, our motorsports quiz website stands as a testament to the enduring passion for racing, providing a virtual pit stop where fans can test their knowledge and share the thrill of the track.
